Project Guidelines

In order to successfully complete your interdisciplinary project, please ensure your team adheres to the following guidelines:

  • Teams must be comprised of 5-6 students.
  • Each team must include 2 CS students, 2 EC students, and at least 1 ME student.
  • Teams cannot be formed within the same branch or cluster.
  • Ensure all contact information provided is up-to-date.
  • The site is intended solely for team formation. Please refrain from misusing personal information.

This platform is designed to streamline team formation, making it easier for students to connect based on their clusters and branches.
