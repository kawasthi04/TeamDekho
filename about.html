Interdisciplinary Project Guidelines (XX367P)

VI Semester BE students must complete an Interdisciplinary Project for 3 credits. Click to expand each section:

  • Teams: 5–6 students (CS, EC, ME)
  • No same branch/cluster groups
  • Suggested: 2 CS, 2 EC, 1–2 ME
  • Three guides (one per cluster)
  • Topic per college-approved list
  • Outcome: Prototype, proof-of-concept, or paper
  • Meet guides twice weekly
  • Maintain a project log
  • Evaluation in three phases (20%, 40%, 40%)
  • Topic & synopsis: 08/04/2025
  • Methodology & results: 26–28/05/2025
  • Final presentation: 07–09/07/2025
  • Register on the Join page
  • Post vacancies if you need team members
  • Keep contact info updated
  • Use the platform responsibly
